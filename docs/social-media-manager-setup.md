# Social Media Manager Setup Guide

Auto-post your video clips to multiple platforms from one dashboard.

## Which Tool Should You Choose?

| If you want... | Choose |
|----------------|--------|
| Simplest setup, cheapest | **Buffer** |
| Auto-recycle content, AI help | **SocialBee** |
| Best for Instagram/visual | **Later** |

This guide covers **Buffer** and **SocialBee** - the two best options for posting dharma/meditation content.

---

## Option 1: Buffer (Simplest)

Best for: Beginners who want easy scheduling without complexity.

### Pricing
- **Free:** 3 channels, 10 scheduled posts per channel
- **Essentials:** $6/month per channel, unlimited posts
- **Team:** $12/month per channel, collaboration features

### Step 1: Sign Up

1. Go to [buffer.com](https://buffer.com)
2. Click **Get Started Now**
3. Sign up with Google, Apple, or email
4. No credit card required for free tier

### Step 2: Connect Your Platforms

From the dashboard:

1. Click **Channels** in the left sidebar
2. Click **Connect Channel**
3. Select platform and authorize:

| Platform | What You Need |
|----------|---------------|
| Instagram | Business or Creator account |
| TikTok | TikTok account |
| YouTube | Google account |
| Facebook | Facebook Page (not personal profile) |
| LinkedIn | LinkedIn account |
| Twitter/X | X account |
| Pinterest | Pinterest Business account |

**Note:** Instagram requires a Business or Creator account for auto-posting. To switch: Instagram → Settings → Account → Switch to Professional Account.

### Step 3: Create a Posting Schedule

1. Click on a connected channel
2. Go to **Settings** → **Posting Schedule**
3. Set days and times to auto-post
4. Buffer will post your queued content at these times

**Suggested schedule for dharma content:**
- Instagram: 11am, 7pm
- TikTok: 10am, 2pm, 8pm
- YouTube Shorts: 12pm, 6pm
- Facebook: 9am, 1pm

### Step 4: Add Content to Queue

1. Click **Create** → **New Post**
2. Select which channels to post to
3. Upload your video clip (from Opus Clip)
4. Write your caption
5. Add hashtags
6. Click **Add to Queue** or **Schedule Post**

**Pro tip:** Write one caption, then customize per platform using the channel tabs.

### Step 5: Manage Your Queue

- View all scheduled posts in **Queue** tab
- Drag and drop to reorder
- Click any post to edit
- Posts auto-publish at scheduled times

### Buffer Workflow

```
Opus Clip exports clips
        ↓
Upload to Buffer
        ↓
Write caption, add hashtags
        ↓
Add to queue
        ↓
Buffer auto-posts at scheduled times
```

## Option 2: SocialBee (Smarter)

Best for: Creators who want AI help and automatic content recycling.

### Pricing
- **Bootstrap:** $29/month - 5 profiles, 1 user
- **Accelerate:** $49/month - 10 profiles, 1 user
- **Pro:** $99/month - 25 profiles, 3 users
- **14-day free trial** (no credit card)

### Why SocialBee for Dharma Content?

1. **Evergreen recycling** - Your best clips automatically repost
2. **AI Copilot** - Generates captions and strategy
3. **Content categories** - Organize by theme (meditation, quotes, teachings)
4. **Variations** - Auto-creates caption variations to avoid repetition

### Step 1: Sign Up

1. Go to [socialbee.com](https://socialbee.com)
2. Click **Start Free Trial**
3. Sign up with email
4. No credit card required for trial

### Step 2: Connect Platforms

1. Go to **Social Profiles** in sidebar
2. Click **Add Profile**
3. Select platform and authorize:
   - Instagram (Business/Creator)
   - TikTok
   - YouTube
   - Facebook Page
   - LinkedIn
   - Twitter/X
   - Pinterest
   - Google Business Profile

### Step 3: Create Content Categories

This is SocialBee's killer feature. Organize content by type:

1. Go to **Content Categories**
2. Click **Add Category**
3. Create categories like:

| Category | Purpose | Post Frequency |
|----------|---------|----------------|
| Dharma Quotes | Short wisdom clips | Daily |
| Meditation Tips | Practical guidance | 3x/week |
| Story Clips | Personal stories | 2x/week |
| Q&A | Questions answered | 1x/week |
| Evergreen | Best performing clips | Auto-recycle |

### Step 4: Set Up Posting Schedule

1. Go to **Schedule**
2. For each platform, assign time slots to categories
3. Example schedule:

**Instagram:**
- Mon 11am: Dharma Quotes
- Tue 11am: Meditation Tips
- Wed 11am: Story Clips
- Thu 11am: Dharma Quotes
- Fri 11am: Q&A

SocialBee automatically pulls from each category at the scheduled time.

### Step 5: Add Content

1. Go to **Content** → select a category
2. Click **Add Post**
3. Upload your video
4. Write caption (or click **AI Generate**)
5. Click **Save**

**Evergreen content:**
- Check "Evergreen" to auto-recycle
- SocialBee reposts it after all other content in category is used
- Great for timeless teachings

### Step 6: Use AI Features

**AI Caption Generator:**
1. Upload video
2. Click **Generate with AI**
3. Select tone (inspirational, educational, casual)
4. Edit as needed

**AI Copilot:**
1. Go to **AI Copilot**
2. Answer questions about your brand/audience
3. Get a full content strategy generated

### SocialBee Workflow

```
Opus Clip exports clips
        ↓
Upload to SocialBee by category
        ↓
AI generates captions
        ↓
Mark best clips as "Evergreen"
        ↓
SocialBee auto-posts and recycles forever
```

## Comparison: Buffer vs SocialBee

| Feature | Buffer | SocialBee |
|---------|--------|-----------|
| **Price** | $6/mo per channel | $29/mo for 5 channels |
| **Free tier** | Yes (limited) | Trial only |
| **Ease of use** | Very easy | Moderate learning curve |
| **AI captions** | No | Yes |
| **Auto-recycle content** | No | Yes |
| **Content categories** | No | Yes |
| **Best for** | Simple scheduling | Hands-off automation |

**Bottom line:**
- **Buffer** if you want simple and cheap
- **SocialBee** if you want "set and forget" with recycling

## Platform-Specific Tips

### Instagram Reels
- 9:16 vertical format
- 30-90 seconds performs best
- First 3 seconds need a hook
- Use 3-5 relevant hashtags
- Post 1-2x daily

### TikTok
- 9:16 vertical
- 15-60 seconds ideal
- Hook in first second
- Trending sounds help (but not required for talks)
- Post 1-3x daily

### YouTube Shorts
- 9:16 vertical
- Under 60 seconds
- Good for searchable content
- Use keywords in title
- Post 1x daily

### Facebook
- Works with any format
- 1-3 minutes okay
- Captions essential (many watch muted)
- Post 1-2x daily

## Troubleshooting

**Instagram won't connect?**
- Must be Business or Creator account
- Must be connected to a Facebook Page
- Try disconnecting and reconnecting

**TikTok posts failing?**
- Check video is under 10 minutes
- Ensure 9:16 aspect ratio
- Re-authorize if needed

**Videos not uploading?**
- Check file size limits (varies by platform)
- Try compressing video
- Use MP4 format

**Captions not showing?**
- Burn captions into video (Opus Clip does this)
- Or use platform's auto-caption feature
